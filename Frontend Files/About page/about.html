About CampusBite

We're transforming the way students experience campus dining, one order at a time.

Our Story

CampusBite was born from a simple observation: students were spending too much time waiting in cafeteria lines when they could have been studying, socializing, or pursuing their passions.


Founded in 2025 by a group of college students who faced these challenges firsthand, CampusBite set out to build a solution that would benefit the entire campus community. We believed technology could bridge the gap between hungry students and quality campus food.


We’re just getting started, but our vision is clear—save time, reduce stress, and make mealtimes smoother for every student. With constant feedback from our peers, CampusBite continues to grow and improve day by day.
